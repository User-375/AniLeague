Promotion System – Seal of Truth

To rank up from E to C, complete quests or earn the required number of Seals of Truth (votes):

  • 7 votes from 1 rank above
  • 5 votes from 2 ranks above
  • 3 votes from 3 ranks above

From C upward: promotion is only by votes and nomination.

Quest Board – Path to Senpaihood

E-Rank

  • Introduce yourself
  • React to 5 discussions
  • Watch a recommended anime
  • Post a meme or gif
  • Vote in a poll or event

D-Rank

  • Recommend an anime
  • Attend an event or watch party
  • Share a favorite OP/ED
  • Join a genre chat
  • Help an E-Rank

C-Rank

  • Host/co-host an event
  • Post a review or top 5 list
  • Recruit a new member
  • Help someone complete a quest
  • Start a genre debate

Higher Promotions

B → A: 7 votes from A, 5 from S, or 3 from High Senpai or above.

A → S: Nominated by House, approved by Guildmaster or Vice.

Seal of Truth – Voting Lore

"Only those recognized by the Senpai above may ascend through the Seal of Truth."

Use Seal icons, reactions, or custom emojis to track them. Phrases like:

  • “You have received the Seal of Truth.”
  • “The Council recognizes your journey.”
  • “You are now worthy to be noticed.”
